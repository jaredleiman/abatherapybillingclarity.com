Common Questions from ABA Practice Owners

Is this legal advice or compliance consulting?

No. ABA Therapy Billing Clarity provides AI-generated compliance intelligence — educational information about billing rules, OIG priorities, and carrier policies. It is not legal advice, and you should consult a qualified healthcare attorney or compliance consultant for legal guidance specific to your practice's situation.

How is this different from just reading the OIG work plan myself?

The OIG Work Plan is a starting point, but ABA billing compliance spans dozens of carrier policy documents, CMS transmittals, state Medicaid bulletins, and CPT editorial updates — updated at different times, across different sources. We synthesize all of it, filter for what's relevant to your specific state and payer mix, and translate it into plain English with a single action item. That's the work we do so you don't have to.

What states and carriers does the report cover?

All 50 states for Medicaid tracking. Commercial carrier coverage includes UnitedHealth, Aetna, Cigna, BCBS, and Tricare. You tell us your state and which carriers you bill when you subscribe, and your monthly report is tailored to that specific combination.
